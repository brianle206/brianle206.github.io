About Me

At the age of 10 a young man unbeknownst to him what the future had in store, for he would one day become one of the greatest planeswalkers to ever roam the planes. The boy stole away in the helms of the Weatherlight as it traveled to Dominaria to fend off the Phyrexians where he would begin his journey of self discovery. Little did he know that his world which he had known will never be the same. The boy now a man had traveled and battled across many of planes, fending off the fearsome Eldrazi and saving precious Zendikar along side with what he now knows as other planeswalkers. His latest feat was aiding alongside Nahiri to vanquish Kozilek from Innistrad. He now travels to the great planes of Renton where his attempts to join forces with The Wizards of the Coast will be his greatest adventure to date. His name is Brian Le and over the years he has accumulated vast knowledge about the game of Magic: The Gathering and various programming languages.
